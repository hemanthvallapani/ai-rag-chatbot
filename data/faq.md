## Products and Pricing

### What is the AI Platform?
The AI Platform is our flagship product that provides an end-to-end solution for building, training, and deploying machine learning models. It features a no-code interface for beginners and advanced API access for experienced developers. The platform includes pre-trained models, AutoML capabilities, and seamless cloud integration.

### How much does the AI Platform cost?
Our pricing is tiered based on usage:
- Starter Plan: $99/month - includes 100 API calls per day, 5GB storage, and email support
- Professional Plan: $499/month - includes 1,000 API calls per day, 50GB storage, priority support, and advanced analytics
- Enterprise Plan: Custom pricing - unlimited API calls, dedicated infrastructure, 24/7 phone support, and custom integrations

### Is there a free trial available?
Yes, we offer a 14-day free trial of our Professional Plan with no credit card required. This gives you full access to all features so you can evaluate whether our platform meets your needs. After the trial, you can choose to upgrade to a paid plan or downgrade to our free tier with limited features.

### What payment methods do you accept?
We accept all major credit cards (Visa, Mastercard, American Express, Discover), PayPal, and bank transfers for Enterprise customers. All payments are processed securely through our PCI-compliant payment gateway.

## Account Management

### How do I upgrade or downgrade my plan?
You can change your plan at any time through your account dashboard. Simply navigate to Settings > Billing > Change Plan. Upgrades take effect immediately, while downgrades take effect at the start of your next billing cycle. No cancellation fees apply.

### What is your cancellation policy?
You can cancel your subscription at any time with no penalties. Your service will continue until the end of your current billing period. We do not offer refunds for partial months, but you'll retain access to your data for 30 days after cancellation to allow for export.

### How do I export my data?
Data export is available through your account dashboard under Settings > Data Management > Export Data. You can export your data in JSON, CSV, or XML formats. Enterprise customers can also request custom export formats and receive assistance from their account manager.
